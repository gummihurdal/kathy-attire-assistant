First Steps

Getting Started

Kathy Atelier Privé is your personal AI-powered wardrobe assistant. It catalogues your clothes, generates outfit ideas using AI, lets you virtually try them on, and even has a private boutique for selling pieces you no longer wear.

1

Create Your Account

Tap Sign In in the top-right corner and choose Create Account. Enter your email and a password (minimum 6 characters). You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link to activate your account.

2

Upload Your First Pieces

Go to Wardrobe and tap + Add Piece. Upload a photo and the AI stylist will automatically identify the item, suggest a name, detect colours, and assign a category. You can adjust anything before saving.

3

Generate Your First Outfit

Once you have at least 2 pieces, go to Outfits, pick a style direction (casual, business, evening…), and tap Create Outfit. The AI stylist will curate a complete look from your wardrobe with styling notes and colour harmony advice.

4

Set Up Your Style Profile

Visit Profile to tell the AI your body shape, height, what you like to emphasise, and what you prefer to avoid. This makes every outfit recommendation more personalised to you.

✦ Pro Tip

The more pieces you upload, the better your outfit recommendations become. Aim for at least 10–15 items across different categories (tops, bottoms, shoes, bags) for the AI to have enough to work with.

👗

Your Collection

The Wardrobe

Your wardrobe is the foundation of everything. This is where every piece of clothing, every shoe, and every accessory lives — beautifully catalogued and ready for the AI to style.

1

Adding a Piece

Tap + Add Piece to open the upload modal. Drag and drop a photo or tap to browse. The AI analyses your image instantly — it detects the item type, suggests a name, identifies colours, and writes a description. Review the details and save.

2

Editing Details

After the AI fills in the details, you can adjust the name, switch the category, add or remove colours, change the brand, add a description, and tag seasons (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter) to help the AI recommend seasonally appropriate outfits.

3

Browsing & Filtering

Use the category tabs to filter by type — Tops, Bottoms, Dresses, Outerwear, Shoes, Bags, Accessories, and Activewear. The search bar lets you find items by name, brand, or colour instantly.

4

Removing Pieces

Hover or tap on any item card and use the delete option to remove it from your wardrobe. Consider listing it in the Boutique instead if it's still in good condition.

Supported categories:

Tops Bottoms Dresses Outerwear Shoes Bags Accessories Activewear

✦ Photo Tips

Photograph items on a flat surface or hanger against a plain background. Good lighting helps the AI identify colours accurately. One item per photo works best — the AI focuses on the main piece in the image.

✨

AI Styling

Outfit Generator

This is where the magic happens. The AI stylist looks at your entire wardrobe and creates complete, curated outfits based on the style direction and occasion you choose.

1

Choose a Style Direction

Select from seven style profiles: Casual (relaxed everyday), Smart Casual (polished but approachable), Business (commanding and sharp), Formal (elegant occasions), Sporty (athletic chic), Evening (dinner and nightlife), or Weekend (laid-back weekend vibes).

2

Add Context (Optional)

Describe the occasion (e.g. "brunch with friends at a rooftop café" or "job interview at a creative agency"). You can also add preferences like "I want to wear my new blazer" or "no heels today". The more specific you are, the more tailored the result.

3

Review Your Outfit

The AI returns a named outfit with specific pieces from your wardrobe, individual styling notes for each item (how to wear it), a colour story explaining why the pieces work together, an accessories suggestion, and a stylist secret — a finishing touch that elevates the look.

4

Save to Lookbook

Love the outfit? Tap the heart icon to save it to your Lookbook for future reference. You can build an entire collection of go-to looks for different occasions.

✦ Get Better Results

Set up your Style Profile first (in Profile) — the AI considers your body shape, height, and preferences when curating outfits. Also, try generating multiple outfits for the same style to see different combinations.

Deep Analysis

Style Advisor

The Style Advisor goes deeper than individual outfits — it analyses your entire wardrobe as a collection and creates a comprehensive style report with outfit suggestions for every occasion.

1

Run the Analysis

You need at least 3 items in your wardrobe. Tap Analyse My Wardrobe and wait 20–30 seconds while the AI catalogues your pieces, analyses your colour palette, finds combinations, matches outfits to occasions, and writes your personal style report.

2

Browse by Occasion

Filter results by occasion — School, Weekend, Sport, Party, Cosy Day, Date, or Evening Out. Each outfit comes with a name, the specific items, styling notes, and occasion-specific advice.

3

Wardrobe Gaps

The advisor identifies what's missing from your wardrobe and suggests key pieces that would unlock new outfit combinations. Think of it as a smart shopping list.

✦ Advisor vs. Generator

The Outfit Generator creates one outfit at a time for a specific style. The Style Advisor analyses everything at once and gives you a full report with multiple outfits across all occasions. Use the Generator for quick daily inspiration and the Advisor for a comprehensive wardrobe review.

🪞

Virtual Try-On

Magic Mirror

See outfits on yourself before getting dressed. Upload a photo of yourself, choose a style, and the AI generates a realistic image of you wearing the recommended outfit — complete with hair styling tips.

1

Upload Your Photo

Drag and drop or tap to upload a full-body or half-body photo of yourself. You can save multiple photos and switch between them. Your first photo becomes the primary one used for try-ons.

2

Pick a Style

Choose from Sporty, Casual, Smart Casual, Business, Evening, Formal, or Weekend. The AI first selects the perfect outfit from your wardrobe for that style, then generates a visualisation of you wearing it.

3

View Your Look

The result shows the generated image alongside the outfit details, individual item names, and a complete colour story. Below the outfit, you'll find hair styling tips specific to the style — recommended looks, techniques, and what to avoid.

4

Share as Story Card

Tap the download/share button to export your try-on result as a beautiful 9:16 Instagram Story card — complete with the outfit name, style label, and Kathy branding. Perfect for sharing your look with friends.

✦ Best Photo Tips

Use a clear, well-lit photo against a simple background. Full-body shots give the best results. The AI uses advanced virtual try-on technology (IDM-VTON) to map your actual garment photos onto your body, then refines the result with AI image generation for a polished finish.

Buy & Sell

The Boutique

A private marketplace for pre-loved fashion. List pieces you no longer wear, browse what others are selling, and communicate directly through in-app messaging. Every listing feels like a personal boutique discovery.

1

Browsing Listings

The Boutique shows all active listings in a clean grid with photos, brand, title, price, and condition badge. Filter by category and brand. Tap any listing to see full details, multiple images, description, and size information.

2

Creating a Listing

Tap Sell Something to list a piece. Upload up to 5 photos (drag-and-drop supported), then fill in the title, select a brand from the list, choose a category, pick the right size (sizes adapt to the category — shoe sizes for shoes, ring sizes for jewellery, etc.), set the condition, enter your price in EUR, and add a description.

3

Adding to Cart

When you find something you like, tap the shopping bag icon to add it to your cart. The cart is accessible from the header at all times — it slides open as a drawer showing all your items and the total price.

4

Contacting the Seller

On any listing detail page, use the message panel to ask questions, negotiate, or arrange the purchase directly with the seller. Messages are threaded per listing.

✦ Selling Tip

Listings with clear, well-lit photos sell faster. Show the item from multiple angles, include close-ups of any details or imperfections, and write an honest description. Mention the original retail price to help buyers understand the value.

Personalisation

Style Profile

Your style profile teaches the AI what works for your body and what you prefer. The more you fill in, the more personalised every recommendation becomes.

1

Body Shape

Choose from Hourglass, Pear, Apple, Rectangle, or Inverted Triangle. Each shape comes with suggested styles that flatter your proportions and specific things to avoid. The AI uses this to select pieces that work best for your body.

2

Height

Select Petite (under 163cm), Average (163–173cm), or Tall (over 173cm). This affects proportions advice — like whether to go cropped or full-length, or how to balance oversized pieces.

3

What to Emphasise

Tell the AI which features you want to highlight — Waist, Legs, Shoulders, Bust, or Hips. The outfit recommendations will favour silhouettes and styling techniques that draw attention to your chosen areas.

4

What to Avoid

Set boundaries: Tight fits, Short skirts, Low necklines, Bright colours, Loud prints, or Cropped tops. The AI will respect these preferences in every recommendation.

✦ Profile Tip

Your style profile is saved locally on your device. If you switch devices, you'll need to set it up again. The profile data is used by the Outfit Generator, Style Advisor, and Magic Mirror to personalise all recommendations.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need an account to use the app?+
You can browse the app in Demo Mode without an account, but to save your wardrobe, generate personalised outfits, use the Magic Mirror, and access the Boutique, you'll need to create a free account.
How does the AI identify my clothes?+
When you upload a photo, the image is sent to Claude AI (by Anthropic) through a secure backend. It analyses the image to detect the type of garment, suggest a name, identify colours, and write a description. You can adjust anything before saving.
How many items do I need for outfit recommendations?+
You need at least 2 items for the Outfit Generator and 3 for the Style Advisor. For best results, upload 10–15 items across different categories — the more variety the AI has, the more creative and complete the outfits.
Is my data private?+
Yes. Your wardrobe photos and data are stored in your personal account and are not visible to other users. Boutique listings are public by design, but your wardrobe, lookbook, profile photos, and style profile are completely private.
What image formats are supported?+
The app supports JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. For best results, use photos with good lighting against a simple background. Maximum file size for boutique listings is 10MB per image.
How does the virtual try-on work?+
The Magic Mirror uses a three-tier approach: first, it attempts IDM-VTON (a garment-to-body mapping technology) to place your actual clothing photos onto your body. If that's not possible, it uses Flux Kontext to redress your photo in the selected style. As a final fallback, it generates a pure AI fashion image matching the outfit.
Can I use the app on my phone?+
Yes, the app is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. On mobile, the navigation collapses into a hamburger menu. All features — including photo upload, outfit generation, and the boutique — work on mobile.
How do payments work in the Boutique?+
The Boutique currently operates as a listing and messaging platform. Buyers and sellers connect through in-app messaging to arrange payment and delivery directly. There is no built-in payment processing at this time.
